My Letter to You

For anyone, 8 months is a long time. Especially for two young people that have their whole lives ahead of them. It's hard to not get jaded, to not wonder if this is worth it. 8 whole months? When we had only been together for about 20 months prior to this? That's a significant amount of time. We'd be in different countries, doing different things with our own sets of worries and stresses. It has not been easy, and naturally, in my opinion, doubts have been raised. Vocally (by you) and internally (by me), we've wondered what the point of this is, IF there even is a point. As time went on we fought and fought and fought until we reached a breaking point (literally). To be honest, I was always against the idea as you know, but I thought we should give it a try.

Things didn't get easier. We still fought but now we weren't even technically together. Periods of peace were followed by periods of turmoil, periods of miscommunication, periods of misunderstanding, anxiety, stress, worry. This wasn't working. For me the tipping point was our anniversary that I so dramatically ruined. I was so sick of this. It didn't feel right. It didn't feel real. It felt like I was losing sight of what you mean to me. That was a wake up call. I don't want to forget what you mean to me. I don't want to just let this waste away. Because I love you. I really love you with everything I have.

I needed to find an outlet. An outlet that reminded me of why I'm doing this. Why I'm trying to slowly better myself. Hell, even why I want to do all this in the first place. I've spent hours poring over this site. This is more of an outlet for me than it is a gift to you. A project I've spent hours on with nothing but you in mind, nothing but us in mind. I've remembered now what I was about to forget. Right now I feel more intensely for you than I have in these past 4 months. It took me fucking up our anniversary for me to wake up but here I am. Ready to face you, ready to love you and nothing else. No arguments, no malice, just love. You're too precious to me for me to slack off and let all this go to waste. I love you Iman.

Now let's pick up the slack and head towards January with anticipation instead of anxiety. Let's work together to get better. Let's prove to ourselves why we've been together for two years. I'm done letting this time and distance get to me. I'm ready to move on from thinking about all the negatives that can happen and being scared and worried. There's nothing to worry about if we love each other and focus on loving each other.

Your sayang,
Russell.
