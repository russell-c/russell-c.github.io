The Movie

Crunch. Crunch. The sound of fresh potato chips being eaten. Nothing captures the senses of a hungry person more than this sound. Welcome to Hunger Games, the tale of one man's journey to hear the promised sound. Shot on the beautiful grounds of NYUAD, this story showcases the perils of being hungry on a dog eat dog campus. Will he satisfy his craving? Will he fail in his quest? Will he even be able to afford the snack he so desperately hunted down? Tune in to see. May the odds be ever in your favour...
