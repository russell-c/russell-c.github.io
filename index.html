This is my portfolio for the Communications Lab Spring 2018 course at New York University Abu Dhabi. Here you can find all the projects we have done in the class, including:

  • a "movie" website showcasing our effort in the 30MFF
  • a sound project that tells a story
  • an interactive comic project
  • a video project featuring a how-to guide
  • an animation project that draws on all the past projects
All the projects were group assignments and could not have been possible without the help of my group members. All the sleepless nights, bad ideas, good ideas, all culminated in this site that no one will ever see besides Craig. Hi Craig :).

A word on Craig: thanks to Craig Protzel for being a cool dude and for all the feedback he gave on all these projects on how we could potentially improve on them, and thanks for making Comm Lab fun!
